About B.L. Verdantix

We believe every home deserves the quiet joy of a living plant — and we're on a mission to make it possible for everyone.

Rooted in a Passion for Plants

B.L. Verdantix was founded in 2026 with a small dream, but its roots go much deeper. This is not just a business, but a legacy — a dream of my grandfather, Bhajan Lal, which I, Karan Chaudhary, am proud to carry forward.

At just 17 years old, while pursuing my B.Tech in Agriculture, I took the first step toward building this vision. Starting a business at such a young age is not easy, but a strong purpose and determination have always driven me forward.

B.L. Verdantix is not just about selling plants; it's about connecting people with nature. We aim to bring greenery into homes, offices, schools, hospitals, and large institutions, creating healthier and cleaner environments everywhere.

Our vision is to become India's largest plant service company and to build a future where every city, every street, and every home becomes a part of a "Green India."
